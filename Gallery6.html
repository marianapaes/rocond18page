  • March 2018

    • Dear Colleagues,

    We have received a number of requests to extend the joint ROCOND & LPVS 2018 paper submission deadline. The organizing committee have now extended the deadline of Regular Papers submissions until April 1st, 2018. Make sure you don't miss this deadline as there will be no further extensions!

    Submit a paper now: https://ifac.papercept.net/conferences/scripts/start.pl

    We recall that the papers already submitted can be, if necessary, re-uploaded until the deadline.

    If you have any questions regarding submissions please contact the Organizers:

    ROCOND: rocond18@gmail.com

    LPVS: ifac.lpvs18@gmail.com

    Best Wishes,

    Joint ROCOND & LPVS 2018 Organizing Committee
